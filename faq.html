Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this website?

Our website aims to educate children about India's rich cultural heritage, including its history, traditions, art, literature, and diverse cultures in an engaging and interactive way.

What age group is this website suitable for?

The content on this website is designed for children aged 6 to 14 years. However, anyone interested in learning about India's cultural heritage can explore the site.

What kind of resources can I find on this website?

You can find a variety of resources, including interactive lessons, fun activities, educational videos, and stories about historical events and famous personalities from India.

How can I contact support if I have any questions or issues?

If you have any questions or issues, you can contact our support team through the "Contact us" page. Fill out the form, and our team will get back to you as soon as possible.
